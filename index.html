About

Swami Vivekananda was a lion-hearted monk, a messenger of Indian spirituality, and a torchbearer of universal wisdom. With his powerful words and fearless spirit, he awakened the soul of a nation and inspired the world to see the unity of all religions. A symbol of courage, compassion, and enlightenment, he taught us to “Arise, awake, and stop not till the goal is reached.”
